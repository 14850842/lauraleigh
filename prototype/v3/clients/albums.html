Fine Art Album

The Fine Art Album comes in a gorgeous linen cover (either grey, wheat or aqua). The albums are presented in a linen box. They are printed on fine art 140 gsm paper creating that artistic handcrafted feel. The standard size is 25cm x 25cm. You can decide on the number of pages you would like for this album, but what is great about the Fine Art Album is that you can get up to 300 pages as the pages are thinner than the Designer Classic Album.

Parent albums in the 15cm x 15cm size are available. You also have the option of softcover brag books which are 10cm x 10cm. The parent albums and brag books will be the exact replica of your 25 x 25 album. Delivery of albums after final approval takes between 2-3 weeks.

Enquire here.



Designer Classic Album

The Designer Classic Album comes with a linen cover (either grey, wheat or aqua) and is presented in a linen box. They are printed on the finest quality paper. The paper here is a lot thicker than the Fine Art Album paper. The standard size is 30cm x 30cm with the maximum number of pages being 80pages / 40 spreads.

Parent albums in the 20cm x 20cm size are available. You also have the option of softcover brag books which are 10cm x 10cm.

The parent albums and brag books will be the exact replica of your 30 x 30 album.

Delivery of albums after final approval takes between 2-3 weeks.

Enquire here.

The size of the prints and box are either 5x7, A4 or A3.
