About Me



I started my photographic journey in 1998 at Florida State University in Tallahassee Florida where I went on an athletic scholarship. I had no idea what I wanted to study, all I was really interested in those days was running (800m was my race).

After much chopping and changing I settled on two majors, psychology thinking that I would go into sports psychology and photography because I always had a passion for photography. Hours and hours were spent in the dark room at FSU developing film and printing pics. I could spend up to 10 hours perfecting just one print! Most of my work was documentary style where I would go out onto the streets and photograph people. A spent a whole semester photographing homeless people (much to my moms dismay). I still have those prints in my office today. The 7 years at Florida State University were some of the best of my life!

Fast-forward a few years…. Once my running career came to a tragic end way too soon due to injury I knew that sports psychology was not for me as I was left with a bitter taste in my mouth. I do however believe everything happens for a reason, as I was able to pick up and make a career out of my other love – photography.

Wedding photography was the most obvious choice for me because I love everything about weddings! I love the moments, the beauty, emotion and effort that goes into the day. I feel very privileged to be able to document a couple’s special day and create beautiful images that they will treasure forever.

I recently changed my business name to Laura Adams due to a very special person in my live – James Adams. It’s the most wonderful feeling when you find the person who you couldn’t imagine living without, the person who encourages you to be the best you can be and supports you through everything you do xo
